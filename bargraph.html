The above bar graph represents the child death reports of different districts of Punjab. Here the data of 2007, 2008 and 2009 is discussed. In Patiala the death rate is higher in the year 2008, but still the graph seems to be not changed much. Where as in Ludhiana the death rate has increased year by year. In Jalandar the graph hasn’t appeared to increase or decrease much, but the death rate of children was high in 2007 and 2009.
